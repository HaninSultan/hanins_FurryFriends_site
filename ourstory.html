About us:

Furry Friends Training and care is a dog care centre that offers boarding, grooming, and day care. We provide healthy and safe supplies, and surround the dogs with a nice and playful environment.

Our Mission:

Furry Friends Training and care's ultimate mission is to take care of the dogs' owners by providing them with a playful environment and other dogs to play with as well as providing services.
